What is Hypnotherapy?

Hypnotherapy is a gentle, collaborative practice that taps directly into the subconscious mind — where habits, beliefs, and emotions are stored. In a relaxed yet focused state, we rewrite limiting narratives and embed healthier patterns that serve your wellbeing.

How & Why It Works

Under guided relaxation, brain‑wave activity shifts toward theta frequencies, opening a window for rapid learning and imprinting. By pairing customised visualisations with evidence‑based suggestion therapy, we bypass critical resistance and accelerate positive change.
